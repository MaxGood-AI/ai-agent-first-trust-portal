# Privacy Policy

This trust portal is committed to protecting your privacy. This page describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

This trust portal does not collect personal information from visitors browsing public pages. If you access a gated compliance report, we collect only your name, email, and company as provided by the organization that granted you access.

## Data Handling

All compliance data displayed on this portal is managed in accordance with our SOC 2 controls. For details, see our [Controls](/controls) page.

## Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us using the email provided in the footer of this portal.

---

# Terms of Use

Access to this trust portal is provided for informational purposes. Public compliance status information is available to all visitors. Gated compliance report access is granted at the discretion of the organization and may be revoked at any time.

The information presented on this portal reflects the current state of our compliance program and is updated regularly. It should not be considered a substitute for a formal SOC 2 audit report.

---

# Accessibility

We are committed to making this portal accessible to all users. The portal is designed to work with screen readers and keyboard navigation.

If you encounter accessibility barriers, please contact us using the email provided in the footer.
